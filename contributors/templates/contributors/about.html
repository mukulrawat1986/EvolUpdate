Welcome to Evolution Update.

Evolution Update is a collaborative effort to bring recent advances in evolution to the public. We believe that evolution in research is exciting and should not just be shared between scientists. However, primary evolution research is very complicated and hard to understand for the average person.

By sumarizing recent advances in evolution, we hope to bridge the gap between scientists and science-enthusiasts. Check our site regularly to see updates on evolution that you will be able to enjoy and share with your friends. We hope you enjoy the content and learn a little evolution while you are here!
